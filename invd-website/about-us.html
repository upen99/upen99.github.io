About Us

Frosty truffle was set up in 2017 by group of college students who wanted to prove to the world that they can do something on their own from the scratch without being dependent of their parents. It was a small and a simple vision to change the world with whatever they can, let to this concept of Frosty Truffle where people can come in and get anything they want.

What we want to achieve

Our vision is to create one stop online platform for their everyday needs. We want to put an end to the traditional shopping experiences where you’ll have to wonder around shops to shops in order to find everything that you would need but instead you could simply visit Frosty Truffle and find all your needs just with the matter of a click but we will be needing lots and lots of support from our customers in order to accomplish the vision we have.

What we want to give back

We also give opportunity to new comers by training them and letting them work with us to accomplish the dream they’ve. We provide different internships and training programs to help people like us who had very limited resources but a huge desire to do something on their own. We welcome your unique ideas and invest on it so you can achieve your dream career. We not only want to gain profit but want to make a change in the world and help the youngsters get to the goal that they’ve set. So let’s put a hands together and find success in this vast competitive business world, remember we’re always with you for support.
